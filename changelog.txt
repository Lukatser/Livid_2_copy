QoL
-removing rivals is free and has a 15 year cooldown
-abandon PU is free
-Papal Subjugation, Occupation of Rome, Counter Revolution, and Rapid Collapse of society triggered modifiers removed
-Return Rome Papal event will give no penalties for refusing, and AI will always refuse
-minimum relations required to ally or support independence changed to -200
-no dishonorable peace out penalty
-no prestige or dip rep penalty on refusing CTA
-abdicate and disinherit prestige loss lowered to 25
-minimum legitimacy to abdicate lowered to 50
-prestige loss from abandoning a core removed
-prestige gain from sharing maps reduced to +5 and prestige hit for buying maps reduced to -5
-removed stab hit for removing a royal marriage
-time for full occupation reduced from 5 years to 2
-increased max warscore from wargoal from 25 to 33
-increased ticking warscore from +0.4 to +0.66/month
-removed Encourage Development edict and added -10% dev cost to all provinces in states to compensate
-can seize crownland during war
-added option to replace spawning of Kalmyk with rebel stacks
-added option to replace spawning of pirate nations with a rebel stack
-Institution Spread age bonus gives an additional 25% religious unity
-age goal for 90 absolutism reduced to 50 absolutism
-age bonus for -50% change rival cost changed to -15% province warscore cost
-added peace deal option to recolor enemy nations for 5 years

-dev cost penalty from arid reduced to 5%; from arctic to 25%
-doubled institution progress per dev click
-Sale of Titles gives 1.0x yearly income in all ages, instead of the 2.5x in the age of discovery (2.0 for special govs)
-burgher and jain loans are now 3% IPA
-increased starting crownland to 40%
-increased base possible advisors to 7
-buffed Centralization edict to -0.1 autonomy/month
-decreased edict cooldown to 1 month
-removed extra stability cost while in regency
-development required to upgrade CoT's lowered to 5 and 15
-gave equal bonuses (level 4) to all levels of Defender of the Faith
-lowered cost of and tech requirement for canals
-lowered warscore from monuments from +33% to +10%
-lowered stability penalty of Peasants' War to +25% and removed additional progress from bankruptcy
-decreased culture conversion time to 6 months per dev
-added a porcupine event chain
